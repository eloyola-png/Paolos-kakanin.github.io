How You Can Order

Welcome to Paolo's Kakanin, your go-to spot for authentic Filipino delicacies in Roxas City. Founded with a passion for traditional flavors, we started with just one puto and grew into a beloved local favorite. Our menu features a delightful array of kakanin like bibingka, sapin-sapin, and cassava cake, alongside popular dishes like spaghetti and carbonara to satisfy every craving.

At Paolo's, we pride ourselves on using fresh, high-quality ingredients and preparing everything with love and care. Whether you're looking for a sweet treat or a hearty meal, we're here to bring joy to your table. Visit us today and experience the heart of Filipino cuisine!
